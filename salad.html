Quinoa Salad

Try out this colorful and nutritious quinoa salad recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, cucumbers, cherry tomatoes)
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh herbs (such as parsley, mint)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, mixed vegetables, and chopped herbs.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss to combine.
  4. Adjust seasoning to taste. Serve the quinoa salad chilled.

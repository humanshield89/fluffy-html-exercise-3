Green Smoothie

Blend up this refreshing and nutritious green smoothie in minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup spinach leaves
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 1 cup almond milk

Instructions:

  1. Place the spinach, banana, frozen mango, and almond milk in a blender.
  2. Blend on high until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy your revitalizing green smoothie!

Avocado Toast

Enjoy this simple and satisfying recipe for avocado toast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices whole grain bread
  • Sea salt, to taste
  • Red pepper flakes, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Toast the slices of whole grain bread until they are golden and crisp.
  2. Cut the ripe avocado in half and remove the pit. Scoop the avocado flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork until it reaches your desired level of creaminess.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly onto the toasted bread slices.
  5. Sprinkle sea salt and red pepper flakes on top, to taste.
  6. Enjoy your delicious and healthy avocado toast!
